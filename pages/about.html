About Watts App

Converting Microwave Times Between 700 watts and 1100 watts

This website was created to provide a tool to convert microwave times between 700 watt and 1100 watt microwave ovens. I often found microwave packaging only had times for 1100 watt microwaves, but my apartments would only have a 700 watt microwave. The current solutions to this problem did not have an intuitive interface to figure out this information quickly.
